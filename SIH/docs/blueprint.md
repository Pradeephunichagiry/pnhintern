# **App Name**: EcoChampion

## Core Features:

- Dashboard Overview: Provide a consolidated dashboard view for students to track active eco-challenges, points, badges, and leaderboard rank, and a dashboard view for teachers to view challenges and submissions.
- Evidence Submission Analyzer: Use a tool to automatically assess student submissions (photos, videos, text) for eco-challenge completion, flagging potential issues for educator review.
- Challenge Participation and Tracking: Enable students to accept and complete tasks, upload evidence, and track submission status with clear indicators for pending, approved, or rejected submissions.
- Gamified Progress System: Implement a points and badge system to reward participation, unlock levels, and track streaks, fostering friendly competition through a leaderboard.
- Learning Resource Access: Integrate a module providing access to concise guides and eco-learning content, explaining the relevance of each challenge.
- Personal Progress Visualization: Offer a personal progress tracker and achievements portfolio (eco-CV) for students to visualize their contributions and achievements.
- Challenge Creation and Management: Allow educators to create, edit, and assign eco-challenges to classes, schools, or open groups, setting points and deadlines.

## Style Guidelines:

- The theme of sustainability evokes colors found in nature, therefore the background color is a very light desaturated green: #F0FAF5
- The primary color is a vibrant leafy green: #86B300, standing for growth and nature.
- The accent color is a brighter, earthier yellow-green (#A6B300).
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif providing a modern yet friendly and approachable feel for all users.
- Utilize clear and intuitive icons, in a 'flat' style that are related to sustainability, education, and gamification.
- Design a clean, organized layout with a focus on user-friendly navigation. Prioritize key information and calls to action on the dashboards for both students and educators.
- Employ subtle animations and transitions to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
